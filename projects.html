ev trip planner

EVs come with 2 main challenges; limited range and scarcity of chargers outside of cities. EV Trip Planner helps plan a detailed itinerary for long-distance EV journeys by taking into account the vehicle's total battery capacity, energy efficiency, and charging/discharging characteristics. The app can be used all around the world. Built using React, Material UI, and HERE Maps API.
